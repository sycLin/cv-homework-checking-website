  • errorWhy am I getting a False result?

    There are many potential reasons of getting a False reulst.
    1) You're using a .jpg format which inadvertently compressed your resulted image.
    2) Your order of scanning is NOT: left=>right, top=>bottom. (important for recursive operators)
    3) You got your algorithm wrong...QAQ
    4) I'm wrong... and please email me if you think this option applied.
